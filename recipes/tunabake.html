Tuna Bake

Home

Description

this is my high protein and macro friendly tuna bake recipe for my fellow lifters. the recipe itself will have up to 5 servings and conveniently cover your meal prep!

Ingredients

  • 500g pasta of choice - i prefer penne or shell pasta
  • 700g chunk style tuna
  • 1 leggos tuna bake sauce
  • veggies: (up to preference)
    • 4 garlic cloves
    • 1 large onion
    • 1/2 capsicum
    • 1/2 cup sliced eggplant
  • 1 cup shredded low fat cheese

  4. pan fry veggies on medium-low heat with extra-virgin olive oil and season
    • example seasoning:
    • salt
    • pepper
    • garlic powder
    • paprika
    • oregano
  5. when veggies are soft: add drained tuna
  6. after tuna is a bit golden: add tuna bake sauce
  7. optional: season pasta sauce further
  8. add pasta (and 1/2 cup pasta water) to sauce and mix well
