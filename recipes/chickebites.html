Southern Fried Chicken Bites Recipe

Home

Description

This is my high protein and macro friendly southern fried chicken bites recipe for my fellow lifters. The recipe itself will have up to 5 servings and conveniently cover your meal prep!

Macros (per 100g)

200kCal, 25-30g protein, 10g carbs, 3-5g fat

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of lean chicken breast fillets
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • salt
  • pepper
  • paprika
  • garlic powder
  • cooking spray (optional)

Steps

  1. dice chicken breast fillets into bite sized cubes
  2. toss chicken in eggwash with salt a paprika
  3. mix flour, panko, salt, pepper and garlic powder
  4. coat chicken with dry coat mix
  5. air fry for 10 minutes at 200Â°C, make sure to flip at halfway
  6. rest for 2 minutes and serve
